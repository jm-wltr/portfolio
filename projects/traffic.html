ML Model for Traffic Crashes

Predicting high-risk areas in Chicago.

Trained and tuned ML models with 900,000 traffic accidents in Chicago to predict high-risk areas.

Insights

  • Correlated weather, time of day, and location for risk assessment.
  • Evaluated using Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost.
  • Extensive data preprocessing and feature engineering.

Tech Stack

  • Python, Scikit-learn, XGBoost
  • Pandas, NumPy
  • Data Visualization
