Overview

This work focuses on translating simulated acoustic measurements into reconstructions that help characterize internal wall structure. The pipeline bridges physics-based simulation and learned inference to enable fast experimentation.

What I Worked On

  • End-to-end data generation loop: wall geometry creation, acoustic wave simulation, and dataset assembly.
  • Model training and validation in PyTorch, with iterative refinement based on reconstruction quality.
  • Automation that scaled experiments from a handful of runs per day to ~200.

Key Contributions

  • Automated wall geometry generation (Python) and acoustic wave simulations (COMSOL + Java).
  • Scaled simulation throughput from a few runs/day to ~200.
  • Achieved ~93% validation accuracy on simulation data, enabling experiments with real walls.

Tech Stack
